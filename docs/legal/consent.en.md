# Consent — MeteorPointer / Bolidozor

Version 1.0 · Data license: CC0 1.0 (public domain)

By connecting the app to the MeteorPointer API service you agree to the following:

1. **Data sent.** The app sends observation data: altitude and azimuth angles of the
   meteor trail's start and end, time, measurement location, measurement quality, and
   parameters you enter (brightness, colour, shower, note). It also sends the device's
   cryptographic key. It does not send your name, e-mail, or phone.

2. **Identity.** The device acts under a cryptographic key stored on the phone. No
   personal data is attached to the data.

3. **License.** You release the observation data under CC0 1.0 into the public domain.
   You waive all copyright and related rights in it to the extent permitted by law.
   Anyone may freely use, modify and distribute it, including commercially, without
   attribution. For data already sent this release is irrevocable.

4. **Purpose.** The data serves scientific research of meteors and fireballs and is
   shared publicly within the Bolidozor network.

5. **Erasure.** You can delete your device and its data at any time in the app. Deletion
   removes the device identity and the link to your observations. Observations already
   sent remain in the dataset with no link to the device.

6. **Age.** You confirm you are at least 16 years old. The app is not intended for
   persons under 16.

By accepting you grant this consent. We record the document version and time against
your device.
